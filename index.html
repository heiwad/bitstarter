Personal Teleporter tours are an excellent way to immerse yourself in a far away destination without the hassles and expense of airline travel. Our local experts will take you on a virtual walking tours of historic locations and share their insights. Unlike other virtual tours, our tours are live and interactive! You can direct your your own robot to look at anything on the tour path and ask your tour guide questions! You can even chat with othe members of your group just like you would on a real guided tour! Personal Teleporter puts you in charge!

We plan to launch a pilot program in New York City by Spring 2014 and we need your help! We will use the proceeds of the Bitstarter campaign to buy robots from Double Robotics and operate the business while delivering on preorders.

FAQ

What cities will be available?
We are launching our pilot program in New York by Spring of 2014. We will be expanding across the US and Europe after that.
How long is each tour?
We expect to have a variety of tours ranging between 30 and 90 minutes.
Do I need a travel Visa?
Since no actual travel is involved, no Visa is necessary!
Will tours be available in languages other than English?
Yes, we expect to support English, German, Russian, and Arabic.
Will you support flying robots?
We are investigating drones for future expansion but they will not be available during the pilot program.
